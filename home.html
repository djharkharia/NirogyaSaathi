Who are we

NirogyaSaathi is an online platform for all your health queries. We provide you virtual consultancy with the country's top doctor for free. We also maintain your health record to assist you anytime in the future.

Sign up today

How does it work.

You just need to sign-up/login to the platform with your email id and phone number. Then click on the 'Consult for free' tab on the navigation bar, submit your query , provide basic details about the disease or symptoms  and can correspondingly fill a history if any. Then our team will connect you in 12 hours and set-up your appointment with the best doctor available.
Please note that these details need to be filled correctly because our doctor will connect you through these details only.

Orthopedic trauma during ongoing COVID 19 pandemic.

Covid-19 pandemic has shown its massive impact on almost all aspects of human existence including social, economic and healthcare. Trauma victims are one such group of patients who have fallen prey to this worldwide crisis. Paucity of literature, lack of specific guidelines and prevailing panic has created new hurdles for those managing these patients. We performed this observational of first two months of this pandemic, to assess its impact on patient’s attendance and management, in view of novel challenges imposed due to pandemic scenario. Also, we attempted to formulate a pathway to guide management of Orthopaedic trauma patients.

A Rare Case of Ipsilateral “Floating Ileum” with Floating Hip and Floating Knee.

In the light of available data, it can be safely concluded that floating ileum, floating hip and floating knee injuries are not simple skeletal injuries but often a part of polytrauma with other significant associated systemic injuries. The clinical outcome of these patients not only depend on proactive and optimum management of tibial, femoral and ileuml fractures but also on the management of associated injuries.
